# **App Name**: Tareeqal Haqq Store

## Core Features:

- Product Listing: Display a catalog of products available for purchase, mirroring the visual style of rahmaniyyah.com and tareeqalhaqq.org.
- Product Details Page: Show detailed information for each product, including descriptions, images, pricing, and available sizes/colors.
- Shopping Cart: Allow users to add products to a shopping cart, view cart contents, and update quantities.
- Checkout Process: Guide users through a secure checkout process, including shipping address, payment options, and order confirmation.
- Printify Integration: Automatically send orders to Printify for fulfillment and printing.
- User Account Management: Allow users to create accounts, manage their profiles, view order history, and save shipping addresses.
- AI-Powered Product Recommendations: Suggest related products based on browsing history. The AI tool selects a reasonable set of suggestions for similar items to display to the user.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep forest green (#228B22), evoking nature, growth, and trustworthiness. Consider the green color themes from rahmaniyyah.com and tareeqalhaqq.org.
- Background color: Very light moss green (#F0F8E6) for a soft, natural feel.
- Accent color: Muted gold (#B8860B) to highlight key elements and calls to action, signifying tradition and value.
- Body and headline font: 'Alegreya', a serif font with an elegant, intellectual, contemporary feel
- Use simple, elegant icons inspired by Islamic art and symbolism.
- Maintain a clean, uncluttered layout with plenty of white space, inspired by the designs of rahmaniyyah.com and tareeqalhaqq.org, emphasizing readability and ease of navigation.
- Subtle transitions and animations to enhance user experience without being distracting.
